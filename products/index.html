1. Adzaps

What is it?                                                                                                    

Tool for YouTube advertisers for narrowing placement targeting using AI

Why should I use it? 

If you're a SaaS business owner, e-commerce, or anyone who sells products or services online, chances are you'd use YouTube advertising. Advertisers often spend tons on audience targeting and leave out placement targeting. Imagine placing your product ad just before a video which is actually related to your product, that's the beauty of placement targeting. And there are ways to reach out to your audience much faster using growth and historical metrics. 

Why can't I use Google ads?

Google ads is fantastic for audience targeting and provides option to add placement targeting. That's where Adzaps comes in, to provide you best placements. 

Is it ready?

We're in closed beta, finding out product-market fit. 

Where can I find it?

2. HitLyst for Twitter(Shelved for now)

HitLyst achieved more than 25k downloads. 
What is it? 

iOS Twitter client for cleaner timelines 

Why should I use it? 

After following 100+ accounts on Twitter, your timeline is a mess. HitLyst provides interest based curated accounts so you can access cleaner timelines by a single tap. 

Why can't I use Twitter ? 

You can. It's tiring to search and add accounts one by one. HitLyst provides curated accounts by interest and country. 

Nah, I'm good with Twitter. Unless you have something else....
  1. Follow city and country based Twitter trends
  2. Get notifications about trends at regular intervals without opening the HitLyst app
  3. Access to curated News feed and Most popular feed directly in Home screen   
  4. Customise appearance by font, line spacing etc
  5. Quick Swipe on tweets to like, retweet and reply
