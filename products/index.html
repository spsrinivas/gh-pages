1. Adzaps

What is it?                                                                                                    

YouTube ads placement targeting tool

Why should I use it? 

Automated ad targeting of YouTube is a hit-or-miss, wasting ad-spend. Ads end up showing to the wrong crowd. Placement targeting puts your ads in front of viewers already watching videos & channels related to your product or service. Think: showing your cooking ad on a recipe channel. It grabs attention, sparks interest, and leads to better results – more clicks, more sales, and a happier you.

Why can't I use Google ads?

Google ads is fantastic for audience targeting and provides option to add placement targeting. That's where Adzaps comes in, to provide you best placements. 

Any users?

Yes, many marketing agencies and brands

Where can I find it? 

Adzaps.com

3. HitLyst for Twitter(Discontinued)

 

On January 12th, 2023, without warning, Elon Musk ordered his employees at Twitter to suspend access to 3rd party clients which instantly locked out hundreds of thousands of users from accessing Twitter from their favorite clients. Many other Twitter clients such as Tweetbot, Twitterific etc were discontinued. 

What is it? 

iOS Twitter client for cleaner timelines 

Why did people use it? 

After following 100+ accounts on Twitter, your timeline is a mess. HitLyst provides interest based curated accounts so you can access cleaner timelines by a single tap. 

Why did they use Twitter ? 

You can. It's tiring to search and add accounts one by one. HitLyst provides curated accounts by interest and country. 

What were the other features? 
  1. Follow city and country based Twitter trends
  2. Get notifications about trends at regular intervals without opening the HitLyst app
  3. Access to curated News feed and Most popular feed directly in Home screen   
  4. Customise appearance by font, line spacing etc
  5. Quick Swipe on tweets to like, retweet and reply
